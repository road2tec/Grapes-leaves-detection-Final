🌱 What are Carbon Credits?

A carbon credit = 1 tonne of CO₂ reduced or captured. As a farmer, you earn credits by adopting eco-friendly practices and can sell them in the carbon market for ₹500 - ₹5,000 per credit. India's Carbon Market launched under BEE (Bureau of Energy Efficiency) in 2023.
