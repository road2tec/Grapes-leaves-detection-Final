Classification Categories

Diseases We Detect

Our model classifies grape leaves into four categories with high precision

🟤

Black Rot

Caused by Guignardia bidwellii, creating circular lesions with dark borders on leaves and causing fruit rot.

🍂

ESCA (Black Measles)

A complex fungal disease causing distinctive tiger-stripe patterns on leaves and internal wood decay.

🍃

Leaf Blight

Characterized by irregular brown patches that expand rapidly across the leaf, causing severe defoliation.

🌿

Healthy

A vibrant, disease-free grape leaf — the ideal condition for optimal grape production and vine health.
